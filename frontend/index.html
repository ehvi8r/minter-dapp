About Bored Ape Cannabis Club

 

Bored Apes Cannabis Club is a collection of 9,985 script generated Apes and 15 Hand-created 'Genesis' Apes that are unique in a special way for a total of 10,000 Apes!

 

Each of the scripted Apes is created randomly from a selecion of various backgrounds, tops, glasses, smokeables and I threw in the BACC logo as well. They were given rarity data, so some are more rare than others, and of course the Genesis Apes are unique, each with a special meaning, and there are only 15 of them, each made by hand, and are not script generated. I made one super special just for Justin Beiber (sneak peak below), since I know he owns a Bored Ape! Oh and he's Canadian, and this is kind of a Canadian thing since canabis became legal in Canada a couple of years ago, there is some specific Canadian content in some of the Apes too... maybe some day the US and the rest of the world will follow! There's also another super special Genesis Ape which whick was created for another project, maybe they'll scoop it! The Genesis Apes are up on Opensea and will bea available for purchase soon.
